How can YOU help?

Be My Eyes is a non-profit, put on this planet to make a positive impact on the lives of blind people. We fully rely on donations and volunteers to sustain our operations. So here's how you can help:

Become a sighted volunteer

Be My Eyes could not exist without sighted volunteers. If you have time and an iOS device, download the app and lend your eyes to the blind.

Translate into your language

You can help translate Be My Eyes into your native language. Getting started is easy and unbinding. Click here to start translating.

Donate to the project

To keep our servers running, we face a fixed cost every month. Will you help us meet the cost? Donate via PayPal or Credit Card.
